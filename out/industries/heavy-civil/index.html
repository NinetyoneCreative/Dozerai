The reality on site

Sound familiar?

People on foot, machines in motion

Grade checkers and pipe crews are in the work zone all day, exactly where operators can't see them — behind the counterweight, beside the blade, in the swing radius.

New operators, every phase

Subs rotate on and off the job. A new operator doesn't know the site, the crew, or where the utilities run — and that's when incidents happen.

Disputes you can't prove

Rework claims, damaged utilities, 'who hit what' — without footage it's your word against the sub's, and it costs you either way.

How Dozer fits

One system, tuned to your environment

The same cameras, intelligence, and dashboards — pointed at the hazards that actually matter on a heavy civil site.

01

In-cab alerts before a backover

Computer vision classifies people vs. equipment and warns the operator in real time when a worker is in the danger zone — not after.

360° GPS-tagged record
